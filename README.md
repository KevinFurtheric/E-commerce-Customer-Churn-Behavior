## ðŸ’¡ Key Insights

- Overall churn rate is **~28.9%**, indicating significant retention opportunity
- Younger customer segments exhibit higher churn rates
- Churn increases sharply after **~30 days of inactivity**
- Engagement metrics consistently decline prior to churn events
- Certain regions contribute higher customer volume but lower retention
